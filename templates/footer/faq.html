Frequently Asked Questions

Payment and Cancellation
What methods of payments do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa and MasterCard. We also offer cash on delivery service.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, it is 100% secure.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can do so if your order is not processed yet. For more details on this, check our refund policy.

Order Items
Can I change my order items?

Yes, you can do so for free if your order is not processed yet or for extra charges if it has already been processed. For more details on this, check our refund policy.

I just realized that I ordered the wrong size - can this be fixed?

Yes, you can do so for free if your order is not processed yet or for extra charges if it has already been processed. For more details on this, check our refund policy.

Do you offer refunds or exchange?

We guarantee the quality of all products. We will provide refunds within a few days of the end of the campaign if a product is damaged or significantly different from what is displayed the pictures. For more details on this, check our refund policy.

What if my item is damaged?

We guarantee the quality of all products. We will provide refunds within a few days of the end of the campaign if a product is damaged or significantly different from what is displayed the pictures. For more details on this, check our refund policy.

Shipping and Delivery
When will I receive my order?

Most of our merchandise is made to order. For the preordered purchases, expect to recieve your order within a day of the product release date. For regular purchases, you can expect to recieve the order in a week.

Where is my order now?

Unfortunately, you can not track your oder as of now. But we will be implementing this functionality very soon.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship to most countries internationally except where it is not feasible for us. However, we charge an extra fee on all international shipments.

Can I pay extra for expedited shipping?

We currently do not offer expedited shipping, but we will make sure you get your order as soon as possible!
